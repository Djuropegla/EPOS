Among Us is a game
about teamwork and betrayal.

Impostors try to kill everyone before they finish the tasks.

The Crewmates

The goal of a Crewmates is to complete all tasks without being killed by an Impostor, with the secondary goal of
finding all Impostors and ejecting them off the map. Crewmates who have been killed by An Impostor or ejected become ghosts.

There are 3 randomly acquired roles for Crewmates:

Regular

Engineer

Scientist

Regular Crewmate only works on tasks, while Engineer and Scientist, besides having to work on tasks - have special abilities
- Engineer has access to vents, while Scientist has insight into vital signs of everyone.

The Impostor

The primary goal of The Impostor(s) is to kill enough Crewmates to have an equal amount of Impostors and Crewmates before all tasks are completed.

There are 2 randomly acquired roles for Impostors:

Impostor

Shapeshifter

All Impostors cannot complete any tasks; however, they are given a fake task list as general guidance on feigning a Crewmate's behavior and blending in with them. Shapeshifter can, unlike regular impostor, transform into any living crewmate.
